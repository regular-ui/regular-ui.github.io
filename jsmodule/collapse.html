折叠面板 Collapse

示例

基本形式

<collapse>
    <panel title="Panel1">Content1</panel>
    <panel title="Panel2" open>Content2</panel>
    <panel title="Panel3">Content3</panel>
    <panel title="Panel4">Content4</panel>
</collapse>

每次只展开一个

<collapse accordion>
    <panel title="Panel1">Content1</panel>
    <panel title="Panel2">Content2</panel>
    <panel title="Panel3">Content3</panel>
    <panel title="Panel4">Content4</panel>
</collapse>
